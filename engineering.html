Engineering Experience

Gretchen attened Olin College of Engineering for four years graduating in 2020 with a BS in Engineering with a concentration in Robotics. During her time at Olin she completed many projects both individually and in teams. Gretchen's favorite projects were those that docused on human centered design. On her teams Gretchen filled various rolls, primarily as the project manager.
